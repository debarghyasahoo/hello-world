Brand Tone & Voice

Samsung's brand voice reflects who we are and how we connect with people. All AI-generated content must align with these tone guidelines. Innovative

Samsung leads, never follows. Content feels ahead of its time. We create trends, we don't chase them.

Example: "The future arrived. And it fits in your pocket."

Human-Centric

Technology serves people. Lead with human stories and emotional connection before specs.

Example: "Made for the moments that matter most."

Premium

Aspirational without being exclusionary. Quality and craftsmanship in every word choice.

Example: "Crafted to perfection. Built to inspire."

Direct

Confidence and clarity. No jargon, no fluff. Say it simply, say it powerfully.

Example: "Clearer. Brighter. Yours."

Optimistic

Technology makes life better. Content should feel energizing and forward-looking always.

Example: "The best is yet to come. And Samsung will take you there."
